BIOGRAPHY

Anglo-French quartet Curl are set to release their fourth album, Interferometry. Final mixes and post production work are about to be completed. Photography, text/logo/fonts and artwork are being developed.

To make all of this happen CURL are looking for Professional Management to help guide us through the right choices for this release. We are label free and have a wealth of industry experience but need that guidance and savvy for the current market. We are supremely confident in this record and will deliver onstage to support that. See below for private listening links.

We’d love to hear from you straightaway ... ahead of any release strategies.

Initially an Electronic project started by French Producer and Guitarist Frank Amendola (Corpus Delicti) at the start of the Millennium. Albums Ultimate Station and Inner were released (EMI France). Curl has since developed an international collaboration. Two of the band from from the South of France (Nice and Marseille), two from the South Coast of England (Portsmouth). Since 2009 the music has featured the soaring, jazz influenced vocals and keyboards of Hayley Alker (Helsinki), guitar from Wil Garcia (Teenage Sin Taste), and drums from UK Producer Jonny Callender (Cranes, Vex).

Three albums have followed, We Are Complex (2011), Exit Real Life (2014) and Shapeshifters (2019). This and EP / compilations has been consistently supported by quality ‘Live’ shows across Europe.

In 2015 ‘Award winning’ French film Les Enfants d’Okeanos featured a soundtrack that was exclusively from Curl and from the internationally acclaimed Director Fabrice Begotti.

CURL offer new wave, dreamy productions that will send the listener through an emotional sonic journey. Angelic combinations of twisting guitar chime, fluttering synthesizers and beastly rhythms are married to Goddess like vocal melodies - intense sonic sculptures for the audio palette.
